Why is my dishwasher not working?

Your dishwasher won’t start, what to do now? That can be a big hassle, especially when you have a pile of dishes waiting to be cleaned. Take a look at some possibilities for the cause of this issue:

01. Door Switch or Latch

The door switch and latch may prevent the dishwasher from running while the door is closed if they are defective.

02. Thermal Fuse

The thermal fuse shuts off power to the dishwasher if it overheats. If the thermal fuse blows, it will prevent the dishwasher from starting.

03. Main Control Board

The main control board provides power to nearly every component of the dishwasher. If there is a problem with the main control board, it might not send voltage to the dishwasher, causing the dishwasher not to start.

04. Timer

The timer allows power to flow to the proper parts at the proper times. If the timer isn’t working properly, it might not send voltage to the dishwasher, causing it not to start.

Why isn’t my dishwasher filling with water?

If you start your cycle and the dishwasher won’t fill with water, it is most likely because of the Water Inlet Valve. the water inlet valve opens to allow water to enter the dishwasher. If the water valve is clogged or defective, water will not flow into the dishwasher. Over time, calcium deposits can build up in the water supply valve or in the small filter screen inside the water inlet valve. The water pressure to the valve also needs to be checked.

Why is my dishwasher
not cleaning properly?

Take a look at the most common causes for dishes coming out dirty from your dishwasher:

01. Water Inlet Valve

The water inlet valve opens to allow water to enter the dishwasher. If the valve is clogged or defective, the dishwasher won’t get enough water to clean dishes properly.

02. Wash Arm

There are different wash arms in the dishwasher that circulate water to the dishes. If water can’t flow properly through the wash arm due to a clog or faulty parts, the dishes won’t be cleaned properly.

03. Circulation Pump

The circulation pump uses a motor and impeller to force water up through the wash arms. If the circulation pump is defective, the dishes won’t be cleaned properly.

Why are my dishwasher buttons not working?

If the buttons don’t work, confirm that the dishwasher display is working. If the display is still working, try pressing some of the touchpad buttons. If some of the buttons respond correctly but others don’t, it is likely that the touchpad is at fault.If the dishwasher display is working butsome of the buttons don’t respond correctly, the touchpad and control panel might need to be replaced. Lastly, the main control board will be checked. If there is a problem with the main control board, it might not send voltage to the dishwasher touchpad, causing the touchpad buttons not to work.
